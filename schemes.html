Active Schemes

Currently running government schemes available for farmer enrollment.

Finance

Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-Kisan)

The PM-KISAN scheme aims to supplement the financial needs of Small and Marginal farmers (SMFs) in procuring various inputs to ensure proper crop health and appropriate yields. Under the Scheme, a direct payment of ₹6,000 per year is transferred in three equal installments of ₹2,000 every four months directly into the Aadhar-seeded bank accounts of eligible landholding farmers.

Requirements to Apply

  • Must be a landholding Small and Marginal Farmer (SMF) family.
  • Must have a valid Aadhar card linked (seeded) to a bank account.
  • Completion of the e-KYC process on the PM-Kisan portal is mandatory.
  • Valid land ownership documents proving cultivable land holding.

Finance

Kisan Credit Card (KCC) Interest Subvention Scheme

Provides adequate and timely credit support from the banking system under a single window with flexible and simplified procedures. It helps farmers meet their cultivation, post-harvest, and other agricultural financial needs with reduced interest rates.

Requirements to Apply

  • Must be an individual farmer, joint borrower, tenant farmer, or sharecropper.
  • Must possess valid identity proof (Aadhar, PAN, Voter ID).
  • Requires land ownership records or proof of tenancy/sharecropping.
  • A passport size photograph and a duly filled application form submitted at the nearest bank branch.

Insurance

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

A comprehensive crop insurance scheme providing financial support to farmers suffering crop loss/damage arising out of unforeseen events. It features an extremely low premium rate to help stabilize the income of farmers and encourage them to adopt innovative agricultural practices.

Requirements to Apply

  • Open to all farmers growing notified crops in a notified area.
  • Must have an active bank account.
  • Aadhar card is mandatory for enrollment.
  • Sowing certificate or land records confirming the crop being cultivated.

Past / Replaced Schemes

Schemes that have been concluded or replaced by newer, more efficient initiatives.

National Agricultural Insurance Scheme (NAIS)

An older crop insurance scheme intended to provide insurance coverage and financial support to the farmers in the event of failure of any of the notified crops as a result of natural calamities, pests, and diseases.

Status: Replaced

This scheme has been replaced by the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) which offers lower premiums and more comprehensive coverage for a wider variety of crops.

Modified National Agricultural Insurance Scheme (MNAIS)

A pilot program introduced to improve upon the NAIS by integrating private sector insurers and offering more precise loss assessment at the village/panchayat level rather than the block level.

Status: Replaced

MNAIS has also been merged into and superseded by the Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) for uniform implementation across the country.
