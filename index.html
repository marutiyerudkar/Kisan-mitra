Discover actionable programs designed to boost your agricultural productivity and ensure financial stability.

Finance

PM-Kisan Samman Nidhi

Income support of ₹6,000 per year provided to all landholding farmer families across the country in three equal installments.

  • Direct Bank Transfer
  • 100% Govt Funded
Apply Now →
Insurance

Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY)

Comprehensive crop insurance scheme providing financial support in the event of failure of any of the notified crops.

  • Extremely Low Premium
  • Full Insured Amount
Apply Now →
Finance

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

Access timely and adequate credit for farmers to meet their comprehensive credit requirements for agriculture.

  • Reduced Interest Rates
  • Flexible Repayment
Apply Now →
Equipment

Agri Infrastructure Fund

Financing facility for investment in viable projects for post-harvest management infrastructure and community farming.

  • 3% Interest Subvention
  • Credit Guarantee
Apply Now →
Equipment/Irrigation

Krishi Sinchayee Yojana

Focuses on improving water use efficiency 'More crop per drop', expanding cultivable area under assured irrigation.

  • Micro-irrigation subsidies
  • Water conservation
Apply Now →
Market

e-NAM (National Agri Market)

A pan-India electronic trading portal which networks the existing APMC mandis to create a unified national market.

  • Transparent Pricing
  • Direct access to buyers
